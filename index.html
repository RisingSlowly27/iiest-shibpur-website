Our Legacy

Pioneering Technical Education for over 160 Years

IIEST Shibpur has stood at the forefront of technical education since the mid-19th century. From its humble beginnings to its current status as an Institute of National Importance, our mission remains unchanged: to empower the next generation of global engineers and researchers.

Our sprawling campus on the banks of the Hooghly River is a vibrant ecosystem where history meets cutting-edge innovation. We provide a rigorous academic environment coupled with a rich extracurricular life, ensuring holistic development for every student.
